Welcome to the Oreopolis mining game!
The goal of the game is to navigate the depths of Oreopolis collecting gems and ores to then sell at the shop.
Not only is your bag storage limited, but the more weight you carry the more energy you will use so make sure you keep
an eye on your energy levels!
